Compost: What to know?

  • What to do with Organic Waste

    Locate a curbside recycling or recycling center in your area. Locate a green trash bin to dispose of your organic waste. Look into starting a compost bin to reduce landfill gas emissions and create enriched soil.

  • Common compostable items

    Common compostable items include: Non-animal food scraps: fruits, vegetables, peelings, bread, cereal, coffee grounds and filters, tea leaves and tea bags, leaves, grass clippings, and eggshells.

  • Local compost guidlines

    Be sure to follow your local compost guidlines, please contact your local waste management center for more information.

  • Fun Fact

    A typical household generates nearly 474 pounds of food waste each year. Almost 90% of that can be composted.
