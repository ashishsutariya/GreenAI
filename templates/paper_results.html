Paper: What to know?

  • How to recycle paper

    Locate a curbside recycling or recycling center in your area. Locate a yellow to dispose your paper. Or view the recycling code on your item for directions on how to dispose your paper.

  • Common paper items

    Common household paper items include: paper boxes, cardboard, milk cartons, office paper, toliet paper rolls, paper towel rolls, and paper bags.

  • Local recycling guidlines

    Be sure to follow your local recycling guidlines, please contact your local waste management center for more information.

  • Fact

    Producing one ton of recycled paper creates 74% less air pollution, 35% water pollution; and saves 17 trees compared to producing one ton of paper products from virgin wood.
